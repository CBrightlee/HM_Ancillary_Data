## Hidden Marsh Ancillary Data Repository
This repository includes data for different years from Hidden Marsh located in Montezuma National Wildlife Refuge, Montezuma, NY. 

### Hidden 2024
* Water analyte samples results from three different water control structures around Hidden Marsh. NE and SE are inlet structures and NS is the structure that runs between the north and south impoundments at the site (they are managed as one wetland).
* Loss on Ignition (LOI) results from random soil samples collected in both the North and South impoundments
